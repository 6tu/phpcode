= Install !WebKit =

'''!WebKit is the developer version of Apple's Webbrowser Safari that is based on KHTML.'''

You can get a nightly build of !WebKit from:[[BR]]
http://nightly.webkit.org/


Put the !WebKit into your Applications folder. (/Applications)

(If you put !WebKit somewhere else than into your Applications directory (/Applications), you may have to setup the path to !WebKit in pici-servers configuration file!)

---------

Webkit is used for rendering webpages in pici-server.
